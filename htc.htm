HTC-(How TO Come)

Train Journey / Nearest Railway Station

Shegaon has a good option for train journey access because being located on the central
railway's important route Mumbai - Kolkatta. All the super fast and express trains halts at
Shegaon railway station.
1)Delhi- Bhusawal and Banglore- Bhusawal; Shegaon is 120 Kms.
About 3 hours road or trains journey from Bhusawal
2) Mumbai- Bhusawal - Shegaon (towards Nagpur).
3)Kolkatta- Nagpur- Shegaon (towards Mumbai/Ahmedabad)
4)Chennai-Nagpur-Shegaon (towards Mumbai/Ahmedabad)

Road Travel / Bus Route :

Shegaon is located close to National highway 5. Buses ply regularly between Akola and
Shegaon, Khamgaon and Shegaon. Various other cities of the state are also connected with
Shegaon on bus route with direct buses.
i)Bhusawal - Shegaon(120 Kms 3 hours journey)
ii) Aurangabad-Shegaon(225Kms 4 hours journey)
iii)Nagpur-Shegaon(300 Kms 6 hours journey)

Air Travel / Nearest Airport :

Shegaon don't have airport for air travel, Akola is the nearest airport.
a)Delhi - Mumbai - Aurangabad, Banglore- Mumbai-Aurangabad (nearestairport) Shegaon is 225 Kms. About 4hours trains journey from Nagpur
b)Delhi-Nagpur,Chennai-Nagpur and Kolkata- Nagpur.Shegaon is 300 Kms. About 6 hours by road or 4.30 hours trains journey from Nagpur.
